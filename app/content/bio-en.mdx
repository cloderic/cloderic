---
title: Biography
---

Clodéric has been building AIs since 2006 with a focus on human-AI collaboration and finding the right balance between autonomy and full supervision. At Inria, Golaem, and MASA Group, he worked on explicit AI techniques applied to video games, simulations, and visual effects. In 2015, he co-founded craft ai, developing explainable machine learning solutions for time series modeling. Since 2020, Clodéric has been leading the tech and research strategy at AI Redefined, where he oversees a platform for creating collaborative systems involving humans and AIs, implemented in multiple products. His work has spanned industries, from empowering artists to control virtual characters in movies to assisting instructors in delivering personalized training simulations and helping engineers optimize energy and transportation infrastructures.
